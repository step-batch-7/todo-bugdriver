    Instructions to use

    1. Add TodoList Enter title of new todoList in Box asking for new Todo.. and press enter or click on add button next to it.
    2. Add Task in TodoList Click on TodoList from left and enter name of new task in right side box asking for New Task.. and press enter or click on add button next to it.
    3. Delete Task in TodoList Click on " - " icon on right most of task to delete.
    4. Edit Task in TodoList Click on" " icon on right to edit.
    5. Set deadLine to Task in TodoList Click on" " icon on right to set deadLine.
    6. Delete TodoList Click on " x " icon on top right of todoList Box to delete TodoList.
    7. Edit TodoList title Click on title of todoList on top todoList Box to edit Title.
    8. Search Tasks and TodoLists Click on" "icon on top right of page to search.
    9. Move Task of One TodoList to another TodoList Drag a taskname to another Todolist and click on green ok button in confirmation box.
    10. Merge Two todoLists. Drag a TodoList from list of todoList on left to another TodoList and enter a new title in popup box it will create a new todo with new title and tasks of both existing todos. It will replace existing both TodoLists with new todoList.
    11. Logout Click on" "icon on top right of page to Logout.
